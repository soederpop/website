04 — A Day in the Life

Real Situations, Handled

These aren't hypotheticals. Every home health care agency deals with exactly these situations every single day. Here's how the system handles each one.

☼ 6:30 AM — Before you're even in the office

Caregiver Calls Out Sick

Rosa texts that she can't make her 8am shift. The system sees the message, checks who else has dementia care certification and is available, texts the top three matches, and updates you when someone confirms. You wake up to "Handled — Maria is covering."

AI + Your Caregivers
☼ 8:00 AM — Start of day

Daily Briefing

When you sit down with your coffee, there's a summary waiting: 12 shifts active today, one coverage gap flagged for Thursday, two certifications expiring this month, yesterday's visits all confirmed. Nothing missed.

AI — automatic
⚠ 11:00 AM — Urgent

New Client Intake

You get a referral call. You jot down the basics on your phone: name, address, care needs, start date. The system creates the client profile, drafts a preliminary care plan, identifies three caregivers who'd be a good match, and queues up the intake paperwork.

AI + You review & approve
↻ Runs every evening

Shift Reminders

At 7pm each night, the system texts every caregiver with their next-day schedule: client name, address, shift time, any special notes. No more no-shows because someone forgot. If a caregiver doesn't confirm, you get an alert.

AI — automatic
↻ Every Friday at 5pm

Weekly Compliance Report

The system pulls every visit log from the week, cross-references with the schedule, flags any missed visits, calculates hours, and generates a report that's ready for your files or for the state if they come knocking.

AI — automatic
↻ Every Monday morning

Certification Watch

Checks every caregiver's certification dates. If anything expires within 30 days, sends them a reminder with instructions. If within 7 days, alerts you directly. No more scrambling when an auditor shows up.

AI — automatic

You review:  Approve the care plan. Pick the caregiver. Done.

The system:  Notifies the caregiver with the address, schedule, and care notes
              Sends Susan an introduction email with the caregiver's name and photo
              Adds Margaret to the weekly schedule
              Sets up visit confirmation tracking starting Monday
              Files everything for compliance
