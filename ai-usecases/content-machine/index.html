Real Situations, Handled

These aren't hypotheticals. Every solo creator deals with exactly these situations. Here's how the system handles each one so you can focus on what you're actually good at — creating.

★ Anytime — ideas don't wait

Shower Idea

You voice-note an idea while getting ready. The system files it as a content idea, researches the topic, drafts a script outline with three hook variations, and suggests the best posting time based on your analytics. By the time you're dressed, there's a ready-to-review script waiting.

AI + You review
▶ Post-upload — automatic

One Video, Five Platforms

You film one YouTube video. The system auto-generates: three TikTok clips from the strongest moments, an Instagram Reel with burned-in captions, a Twitter thread summarizing the key points with a link back, and a LinkedIn post version. All platform-optimized, all ready to review.

AI — automatic
⚡ Breaking — time-sensitive

Trend Alert

A trending topic hits your niche. The system alerts you with context, suggests three angles that match your brand voice, drafts hook variations for each, and clears a slot in your content calendar. You pick the angle, hit record, and you're riding the wave while others are still planning.

AI + You decide
↻ Every Monday morning

Weekly Analytics

Monday morning briefing drops into your inbox: what performed best, what flopped and why, audience growth across platforms, revenue update, and specific suggestions for this week's content focus based on what the algorithm is rewarding right now.

AI — automatic
✎ Batch day — Sunday

Batch Caption Writing

You filmed 10 videos on Sunday. The system generates platform-specific captions for all of them — short and punchy for TikTok, longer with hashtag sets for Instagram, conversational for Twitter. Hashtags optimized per platform based on current performance data. You review and tweak, not write from scratch.

AI drafts + You polish
★ Ongoing — always current

Sponsorship Tracker

Tracks every brand deal in one place: deliverables, deadlines, payment status, usage rights. Auto-generates an updated media kit with your latest stats whenever you need to pitch. Reminds you before deadlines. Flags when payments are overdue.

AI — automatic
