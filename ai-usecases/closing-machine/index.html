04 — A Day in the Life

Real Situations, Handled

These aren't hypotheticals. Every agent deals with exactly these situations. Here's how the system handles each one — so you can handle more closings.

● 11:00 PM — While you're asleep

New Lead Comes In

Someone fills out your contact form at 11pm. The system creates a client profile, sends a personalized response within 2 minutes, schedules a morning follow-up reminder, and pulls up matching listings based on their criteria. You wake up to a warm lead instead of a cold one.

System handles it
● 9:15 AM — Price drop detected

Price Drop Alert

A property your buyer has been watching drops $25K. The system texts them immediately with updated details and asks if they want to schedule a showing. If they say yes, it coordinates with the listing agent and adds it to your calendar.

System handles it
● Ongoing — Every active deal

Transaction Coordinator

Once under contract, the system tracks every deadline: inspection period, appraisal deadline, loan commitment date, closing date. Auto-reminds everyone involved — lender, title company, other agent — at the right time. Nothing slips.

Runs in the background
● On demand — voice or text

CMA on Demand

Seller asks "what's my home worth?" You voice-command: "Generate CMA for 123 Oak Street." The system pulls comps from your market data, adjusts for features and condition, and generates a polished report you can send in minutes — not hours.

You review, system builds
↻ Automatic — every anniversary

Annual Check-in

The system knows the anniversary of every closing. Auto-generates a personalized touch: home value update with current comps, a market newsletter, a just-checking-in message. Your past clients remember you when their friends need an agent.

System handles it
↻ Every Monday at 7:00 AM

Pipeline Review

Monday morning summary: where every deal stands, what's closing this month, which leads have gone cold, suggested follow-up actions for each. One glance and you know exactly where to spend your week.
