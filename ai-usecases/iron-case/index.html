04 — A Day in the Life

Real Situations, Handled

These aren't hypotheticals. Every PI firm deals with exactly these situations every single week. Here's how the system handles each one.

☎ New Client Call

New Client Calls About a Car Accident

Potential client calls in. You jot down the basics: name, accident date, injuries, insurance carrier. The system creates the intake, runs a conflict check against existing cases, generates an engagement letter, and schedules the initial consultation. By the time you hang up, the file is half-built.

AI + You review & approve
⚠ Statute Alert — 30 days out

Approaching Statute of Limitations

The system flags a case that's 30 days from the statute deadline. It auto-drafts a complaint, alerts your paralegal, creates a filing checklist, and puts it at the top of your morning briefing. No case falls through the cracks because someone forgot to check a spreadsheet.

AI — automatic alert
↻ Runs automatically every week

Medical Records Chase

The system checks which providers haven't responded to records requests. It sends follow-up faxes and emails automatically, tracks what's been requested vs. received vs. outstanding, and flags cases where missing records are holding up the demand. No more sticky notes on your monitor.

AI — automatic
✎ Demand preparation

Settlement Demand Package

One command: "Prepare the demand for Garcia v. State Farm." The system compiles all medical bills, builds a treatment timeline, calculates lost wages from the employment records, applies your multiplier, and generates the complete demand package. You review, adjust the number, and send.

AI drafts + You finalize
☼ Every morning at 7 AM

Daily Case Review

Before you've had your coffee, there's a briefing waiting: which cases need attention this week, what deadlines are approaching, which adjusters haven't responded, what medical records came in overnight, and which settlements are ready to close. Ten minutes and you know your entire caseload.

AI — automatic
☶ Before a deposition

Deposition Prep

The system organizes the client's medical records chronologically, generates a complete treatment timeline, flags any gaps or inconsistencies between provider records, and creates a deposition outline with key dates and questions. You walk in prepared without spending the whole night before assembling it.

AI assembles + You prepare
